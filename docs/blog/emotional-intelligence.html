Emotional Intelligence

Here you'll find my reflections and understanding of emotional intelligence

What is emotional intelligence?

My understanding of emotional intelligence is that we can be in control and positively use our emotions in ways that benefit ourselves and others. Understanding our emotions and knowing how to be in control of them can help us to lead more productive, happier, and more fulfilling lives. It can also solidify the relationship we have with others.

The difference between emotional intelligence and IQ?

IQ represents your capacity to solve problems whereas EI represents one's ability to apply and manage their emotions to effectively problems solve.

Why is it important?

Emotional intelligence is important because when one is effective at managing their emotions they can lead more satisfying and fulfilling personal and professional lives. I can deliver health benefits including increased happiness, reduced stress, and improved clarity in thoughts and actions which benefit the individual and those around them. It can contribute to mindfulness, improved attention, and the establishment of positive mental habits. It can also be beneficial for our self esteems and the way we interact with others. It can lift our leadership and reduce the tensions which can creep into our relations with others.
