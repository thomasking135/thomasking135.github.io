Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

"Neuroplasticity provides people with the confidence that it is never too late to learn new things. It provides people with the belief that they can always enhance their skillset and knowledge provided they are driven to do so. The brain is a flexible tool and benefits from regular use just like any part of the body. If it is properly stimulated it is capable of learning new and wonderful things."

Research Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

"A growth mindset is the belief one has in their ability to learn through the effort and strategies they apply to their learning."

"It is relevant because one needs to have the belief that they have the power to learn provided that they practice and place effort into their learning."

In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

"Yes, I think an understanding of the growth mindset has given me the confidence to embrace the idea that I will succeed in my learning provided I place the right effort and energy into my learning."

How will you integrate a growth mindset into your learning journey?

"I think I will do apply a growth mindset to my learning by setting clear and achievable goals. I will set regular challenges and tests for myself to measure my progress and I will learn to embrace struggles as part of my learning journey."
